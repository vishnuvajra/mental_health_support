Q: How can I manage stress in my daily life?

A: It's important to find healthy coping mechanisms, such as exercise, deep breathing, and mindfulness.

Q: What are some tips for improving mental well-being?

A: Prioritize self-care, maintain social connections, and seek professional support when needed.
